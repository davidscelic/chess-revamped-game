Play Chess Revamped Online

Chess Revamped is available to play directly in your browser. You do not need to download anything, create an account, or install an app. Just open the game, choose your side, and start playing.

Play Against AI Now

The current version of Chess Revamped lets you play against Elo-style AI levels from 200 to 2500. These levels are designed for practice and entertainment. They are not official chess ratings, but they help you control the difficulty.

Choose Your Time Control

You can practice with several time controls, including Bullet, Blitz, Rapid, and Daily-style settings. Shorter games are better for fast tactics, while longer games give you more time to understand the changed rules.

Multiplayer Plans

Online multiplayer is planned for the future. The first version focuses on single-player AI practice because it is easier to keep stable, fast, and simple for new players.

A future multiplayer version may include online rooms, matchmaking, player ratings, timed games, guest play, and real-time games against other players.

Why Start With AI?

Chess Revamped has different rules from traditional chess. Practicing against AI first helps players learn how the powerful king, weaker queen, and forward-capturing pawns change the game.
