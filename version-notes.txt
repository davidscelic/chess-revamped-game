Chess Revamped Version Notes

Version: 0.1 Beta
Date: May 2026

Current Features:
- Playable browser chess variant
- Human vs AI mode
- Human vs Human local mode
- Elo-style AI levels from 200 to 2500
- Choose white or black
- Random side option
- Timers for Bullet, Blitz, Rapid, and Daily-style play
- Local rating stored in browser
- Standard and Turkish piece name options
- Multiple board themes
- Legal move highlighting
- Capture highlighting
- Check, checkmate, stalemate, draw detection
- Pawn promotion
- Move history
- Captured pieces display
- Mobile responsive layout
- Game-only page for portal submission

Known Limitations:
- Online multiplayer is not available yet
- AI ratings are practice difficulty levels, not official chess ratings
- Some mobile devices may render Unicode chess pieces differently
- Daily timers are experimental for browser-only play
- No user accounts
- No cloud saving
- No leaderboard yet

Planned Improvements:
- Cleaner custom chess piece graphics
- Better mobile piece rendering
- Online multiplayer
- Leaderboards
- More strategy content
- More puzzle or challenge modes
- Game portal integration
