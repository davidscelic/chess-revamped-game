What is Chess Revamped?

Chess Revamped is a fast tactical chess variant built around one major twist: the king and queen swap movement styles. The king remains the royal piece, but it moves with long-range power like the old queen. The queen becomes a short-range piece that moves one square in any direction.

Pawns are also changed. They move one square forward only, they can capture diagonally forward, and they can also capture straight forward. Castling and en passant are removed to keep the game cleaner and easier to learn.

You can play against Elo-style AI levels from 200 to 2500, choose standard piece names or Turkish-style piece names, and practice different time controls from Bullet to Daily.
